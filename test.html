Welcome to Taniti

Where there's something to do for everybody.

The annual Taniti Beach Festival starts this weekend with live music, local food stalls, and exciting water sports competitions. Don't miss out on the fun! Taniti has opened three new hiking trails offering breathtaking views of the island's lush landscapes and coastal cliffs. Celebrate Taniti's rich history and vibrant culture with a week-long series of events, including traditional dance performances, local art exhibitions, and storytelling sessions. The new Taniti Seafood Market is now open! Enjoy fresh catches of the day, including the island's famous lobster and conch, directly from local fishermen. Taniti is stepping up its eco-tourism game with new initiatives to protect its coral reefs and promote sustainable travel. Join a guided snorkeling tour to see the efforts in action! Discover unique handcrafted items at the Taniti Artisan Fair this Saturday. Support local artists and take home a piece of Taniti's creativity and craftsmanship. Unwind and rejuvenate at Taniti's new seaside yoga retreats. Experience tranquility with sunrise yoga sessions and guided meditation by the waves. Savor the flavors of Taniti with new farm-to-table dining experiences. Enjoy meals prepared with organic produce and ingredients sourced from local farms. The historic Taniti Lighthouse has been beautifully restored and is now open for guided tours. Learn about its fascinating history and enjoy panoramic views from the top. Join the island's legendary full moon parties held on the pristine beaches of Taniti. Dance the night away under the stars with live DJs and vibrant bonfires.
