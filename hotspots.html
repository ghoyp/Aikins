Hotspots on Taniti

Discover the most popular places to visit on the beautiful island of Taniti.

Mount Taniti

Experience breathtaking views and adventurous trails at Mount Taniti, the island's most popular hiking destination. Whether you're an experienced hiker or just looking for a leisurely walk, Mount Taniti offers trails for all levels.

La Mer Restaurant

Dine in luxury at La Mer, Taniti's finest island dining experience. Enjoy fresh seafood and gourmet dishes prepared by our world-class chefs, all while taking in stunning ocean views.

Blue Waters Beach

Relax and unwind at Blue Waters Beach, known for its crystal-clear waters and soft white sand. Perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and beach sports, Blue Waters Beach is a must-visit spot on Taniti.

Taniti Botanical Gardens

Explore the natural beauty of Taniti at the Taniti Botanical Gardens. With a wide variety of tropical plants and flowers, the gardens are a peaceful retreat and a photographer's paradise.
